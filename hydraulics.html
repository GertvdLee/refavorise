Hydraulics Basics

Understanding pressure, flow, and energy in fluid systems.

Hydraulics is the study of fluid behavior under pressure. It’s essential in civil works like dams and pipelines.

Bernoulli’s equation is a key formula:

\ \[ P + \\frac{1}{2} \\rho v^2 + \\rho gh = \\text{constant} \\]

Key Concepts

  • Pressure: Force per unit area
  • Flow rate: Volume per time unit
